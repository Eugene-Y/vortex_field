*In Albert's GR there is no rotation -*  
*So drag here to play and get inspiration*  

Every pair of fluid cells has an instantaneous center of rotation. This is an interactive visualizer of the accumulated instantaneous rotation. You can drag mouse over the Field A to add energy, or inject patterns by double clicking on the Field B.

**Pair distance** sets the annulus center — which cells are paired with each other (as a fraction of grid radius). **Distance delta** sets the annulus half-width — how wide the ring of included pairs is. Narrow delta = thin shell of pairs at a specific distance; wide delta = thick ring. NB: large pair distance × large delta is very GPU intense.
You can use the "sample stride" param to decrease the GPU load.

"Brush size" param applies.
