*In Albert's GR there is no rotation -*  
*So drag here to play and get inspiration*  

Every pair of fluid cells has an instantaneous center of rotation. This is an interactive visualizer of the accumulated instantaneous rotation. You can drag mouse over the Field A to add energy, or inject patterns by double clicking on the Field B.

The "pair range" slider controls which pairs count: slide right to include nearby cells, left for distant ones. At -1 and 1 all the pairs count. NB: this is very GPU intense.
You can use the "sample stride" param to decrease the GPU load.

You can also [inspect](https://eugene-y.github.io/vortex_field/?gridSize=512&dampingLoss=1.000e-9&simSpeed=0.251&brushRadius=34.8&brushStrength=500&brushSpeed=1.00&patternScale=0.430&pairRange=0.0890&sampleStride=1&boundary=1&vorticity=18.0&pressureIters=1&pattern=triangle&velBrightness=40&rotBrightness=70&maskCx=139.00&maskCy=261.95&maskR=34.80) a specific region by clicking on the Field B. "Brush size" param applies.
